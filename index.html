What We Do

We support Hong Kong athletes through…

Start-up Incubation

OnBoard supports local athlete entrepreneurs by providing seed money, skillset training and mentoring. Startups are incubated in form of joint venture and operates under a profit sharing model. In order to maximize its impact, these startups are also required to hired local athletes.

Job Matching & Career Advisory

OnBoard arranges internship and job shadowing opportunities in various fields to allow them to get a taste of different job natures before setting their route for further development.

Freelance Job Opportunity

OnBoard acts as a bridge between skilled athletes and potential employers by providing head-hunting services. From talent scouting to official onboarding, OnBoard provides professional consultation to ensure smooth transition for both parties.

Our Latest News

16 February, 2019 Hong Kong Shared Good Values Case Competition

HKSGV Case Competition aims to stimulate the incorporation of shared good values in Hong Kong’s business sector and inspire prospective business leaders to become driven by Creating Shared Value (CSV). It is our pleasure to be one of the case partners to inspire students to explore the possibilities of supporting local athletes to create their career paths through CSV model. We are looking forward to receiving their brilliant business plans!

28 February, 2019 NewGen

NewGen is a talent platform cooperated by New World Development, McDonald, TownGas and Fung Academy to groom young talents and future leaders. It provides a collaborative training Programme which focuses on  #Intrapreneurship, #Inquisitive, #Innovative and #Impactful as core pillars for the young talent development. We had connected with the talents and knowledge with various industries to think out of the box. We also shared our experiences and insights on #Athletepreneurship.

15 March, 2019 AIESEC

AIESEC in CUHK has kickstarted a Startup Campaign, featuring the theme of ‘Born To Be The Solution’, providing a platform for CUHK students to gain awareness of the milieu of entrepreneurship in Hong Kong and recognise the impact they can create for society. It’s our pleasure to participate as speaker and panellist to share our journey as entrepreneurs with our future leaders.
